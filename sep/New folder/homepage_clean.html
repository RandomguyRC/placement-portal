Comprehensive Platform Features

Our platform digitizes every aspect of campus placement management with role-based access and automated workflows

Role-Based Dashboards

Custom dashboards for TPRs, TPVs, and students with tailored access rights and responsibilities for seamless workflow management.

Automated Duty Management

Intelligent assignment of TPR/TPV duties based on availability and workload, ensuring fair and organized task distribution.

Company Management

Centralized database of visiting companies with job descriptions, eligibility criteria, and important placement dates.

Profile Verification

Manual verification workflow managed by TPRs to ensure only eligible and verified students participate in placements.

Attendance Tracking

Comprehensive logging of student participation in assessments with automated alerts for repeated absences.

Token-Based Support

Streamlined issue resolution system where students can raise queries and complaints that are tracked and resolved by TPRs.
